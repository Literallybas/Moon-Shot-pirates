McMahon Quiz

This interactive quiz is based on the McMahon Score — a clinical tool used to assess the risk of acute kidney injury (AKI) or death in patients with rhabdomyolysis. By answering a series of medical and demographic questions, you can estimate your risk level and receive tailored guidance.

Disclaimer: This tool is for educational and early screening purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice. Your answers will help generate a personalized score based on peer-reviewed clinical research.

Quiz Guide

1. This quiz provides an estimation of rhabdomyolysis complications using the McMahon Score. 2. Be honest in your responses for best accuracy. 3. The questions include age, lab values, and medical history. 4. No data is stored or transmitted. 5. Consult a healthcare provider for clinical concerns.
